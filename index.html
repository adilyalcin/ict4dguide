A GUIDE TO ICT4D FOR EVERYONE

The ICT4D Guide is an open and accessible resource to help field staff in 'information and communication technologies for development' (ICT4D) projects around the world.

READ THE GUIDE

USEFUL

How the Guide Can Help You

Working on digital development projects around the world require ways of thinking, new tools, local insights, and a lot of hard work.

But we don't have to reinvent the wheel.

Flexible

The ICT4D Guide is available to read online, or available to download offline as a PDF, MOBI, or EPUB

Global

These resources, links, organizations, and insights don't stem from one particular time and place—they have been collected from use around the world.

Ready for Launch

Find the right tools easily to launch you on the path of your project—no more starting from scratch.

Collaborative

This guide is the result of collaboration—but it will always need more. Lend us your voice and your assistance.

LIVED

Bringing Experience into Focus

I have worked at the intersection of technology and international development for over 10 years. This is my notebook.

I started the ICT4D guide as a way to both share practices and resources that I've curated in my years of working as a digital development professional, but also to help me explore new paths and gaps in my knowledge with others working in the space.

By working collaboratively to build a simple, straightforward resource that doesn't try to rebuild the wheel but simply points to good examples where that can be done, everybody working in digital development today benefits.
