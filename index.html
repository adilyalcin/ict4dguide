Globally Relevant

Information and Communications Technologies for Development (ICT4D) simply means using technology for good around the world.

Open-Source

The ICT4D Guide is a free and open guide to help anyone who wants to make a positive social impact with technology's help.

Flexible

The ICT4D Guide is available to read online, or available to download offline as a PDF, MOBI, or EPUB
